Calvin the Cat

A Quick Bio

He is from Chippewa Falls, Wisconsin where he was born in Mark and Ursala Whelan's barn.
Soon after his birth, Calvin moved to Rosemount, Minnesota.

Things Calvin Enjoys

Calvin enjoys eating popcorn, butter, and his expensive wet cat food.
He enjoys going outside, bringing dead animals to doors, and taking naps on his owner's neck.

Things Calvin Does That Make His Owners Mad

  • Throws himself at blinds during the night
  • Brings dead animals into house
  • Rubs his face against their face when they are eating
  • Looks like an ugly squirrel when his hair is cut
